All About Z.P. High School Sawargaonüè´

Z.P. High School Sawargaon, established in 1895, is one of the oldest and most respected schools in the region, educating around 900 students from classes 1 to 10. The school has a strong academic track record, with several students scoring above 90% in Class X board exams. Dedicated teachers and an active School Management Committee work closely with the community to promote quality education.

The school boasts important facilities such as a well-stocked library, a computer lab that enhances digital skills, a physics lab, and an experiment lab, all of which provide students with hands-on learning experiences. Participation in the mid-day meal program helps improve student health and attendance. With ongoing government support and community involvement, the school continues to work towards improving infrastructure and expanding educational opportunities for its students.

Z.P. High School Sawargaon proudly operates with 20 well-maintained classrooms and a dedicated faculty of 23 experienced teachers, ensuring personalized attention and quality education for every student. The school‚Äôs commitment to excellence has been recognized with prestigious honors such as the Ideal Teacher Award and the Ideal School Award within the Dharashiv district, reflecting its outstanding academic environment and effective school management.

The campus is known for its cleanliness and hygienic surroundings, providing students with a healthy and welcoming atmosphere. A large playground offers ample space for sports and physical activities, fostering the overall development of students beyond academics. Looking ahead, the school is actively working towards constructing a modern and improved building to accommodate growing needs, aiming to create a future-ready learning environment that supports both academic success and holistic growth.

The school caters primarily to children from farming and economically challenged families, offering quality education from primary to secondary levels. We provide essential facilities such as classrooms, a library, and a playground while actively working to improve infrastructure through community participation and government support.
