Programs Conducted in ZPHS🎉

Zilla Parishad High School, Sawargaon is a proud educational institution located in a rural setting, committed to nurturing the young minds of tomorrow. Our school provides education from Class 1 to 10, aiming to develop not just academic excellence but also moral values, discipline, and life skills among students. Over the years, ZPHS Sawargaon has become a center of holistic learning where students are encouraged to explore their talents in academics, sports, culture, and social service. Our dedicated and qualified teachers work tirelessly to support each student’s growth, both in the classroom and beyond. Recently, we celebrated our Annual Gathering, Science Day, and Library Week with enthusiasm and active participation from students, parents, and staff, showcasing the vibrant spirit of our school. From successful alumni pursuing higher education to top-performing SSC students who make us proud every year, ZPHS Sawargaon continues to inspire and uplift the rural community. Special initiatives like plantation drives, cleanliness campaigns, digital learning programs, and scholarship support have made our school a beacon of positive change. We believe in empowering students through inclusive and value-based education, building not just strong learners but also responsible citizens of the future. As we look ahead, we remain focused on innovation in teaching, better infrastructure, and greater community involvement to ensure our students continue to dream big and achieve more. Stay connected with us as we share stories, achievements, and upcoming events from our school journey.
